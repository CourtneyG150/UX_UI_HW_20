Labs 4 Liberty Website Redesign

Labs for Liberty is a non-profit organization that provides service dogs to veterans in need. The goal of the organization’s website is to raise money through donations and sponsors/partnerships which allow Labs for Liberty to continue providing service dogs to veterans in need.

Interview
Karen

“Highlight stories from veterans that have been touched and benefited from the organizations.”

Interview
Josh

“It would be cool to know the veterans' stories..even just something small about them like their favorite quote - that would make it more personable.”

Interview
Mercedes

“I need to feel more empowered to give this service to our vets.”

The Problem

Labs for Liberty's website is unorganized, confusing, and does not give clear indication of where your money goes when you make a donation.

The Solution

Redesign the website in a way that is direct and specific so no user has unanswered questions.
